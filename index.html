What is special about Calgary?

Calgary Is Known For Being the Best Canadian City! From the world-famous GlobalFest and the storied history of the Calgary Flames to its role as a gateway to the stunning Canadian Rockies, Calgary offers a rich tapestry of cultural, recreational, and economic attractions.

Things to do in Calgary

Calgary Tower

Located 191 metres above the downtown core, the Calgary Tower offers the best view in the city and is a must-see on any visitor's itinerary. On the Observation Deck you'll experience a spectacular 360° view of the bustling city, the majestic Rocky Mountains, the foothills, and the prairies.

Learn more

Calgary Stampede

The Calgary Stampede is an annual, 10-day rodeo, exhibition and festival held every July in Calgary, Alberta. It features one of the world's largest rodeos, a parade, stage shows, concerts, agricultural competitions, chuckwagon racing and First Nations exhibitions.

Learn more

Banff national park

Rocky Mountain peaks, glacial lakes, and adventure come together in Banff National Park - Canada’s first national park and the flagship of the nation’s park system. Extensive hiking trails wrap bright blue lakes, offering breathtaking vistas at every turn. Banff is part of the Canadian Rocky Mountain Parks UNESCO World Heritage Site.
